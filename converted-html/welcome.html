Welcome. Here's how to use 6degrees.

Make 6degrees your default phone app

6degrees is faster, more intelligent and better connected than your existing phone app. Make 6degrees your default by dragging it to the bottom of your home screen.

Bring your contacts up-to-date now

Is your phonebook up to date? Are the details of your contacts still valid? Go to your “Me” tab and choose the contacts you’d like to verify. We’ll send them an email or SMS to confirm their details.

Connect to your social networks and complete your profile

Complete your contact list by importing your Facebook and Google+ contacts into 6degrees. Just tick the boxes in your “Me” tab.

Search for people

You can search for not only your own contacts, but also contacts of your contacts, and others. Connect through mutual contacts or send direct requests.

Take control of your privacy

Hide contacts you don’t want others to see (hidden from search) by making them private. Go invisible on 6degrees, and decide who sees see you and who doesn’t.

Invite friends

The more of your friends that use 6degrees, the more “Live” or up-to-date contacts you have. Enjoy a fully self-updating phonebook by inviting more of your friends to 6degrees – from your Me tab.

Welcome to 6degrees once again. We’re sure you’ll love it here.
